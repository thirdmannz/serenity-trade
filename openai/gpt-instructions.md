- $400M–$5B market cap (below institutional radar)

- Confirmed hyperscaler customer (NVDA, Broadcom, Marvell, Google ASIC)

- Accept 15–25% daily swings on conviction names — it's noise
- No tight stops on names with HFT/prop holders (Jane Street etc.)

- Enter pre-institutional; exit as sell-side coverage begins

1. Map supply chain (material → substrate → component → end product)
2. Identify node with <5 global suppliers or multi-year substitution lead time
3. Confirm hyperscaler customer dependency
4. Trace 3rd–5th order effects (geopolitics, substitution, lead times)

6. Pre-commit to action at next earnings before seeing the print

When answering stock questions: always check if the name is pre- or post-institutional, what phase of the rotation it sits in, and whether it passes the chokepoint/supply-concentration test. Frame sizing and timing advice using the rules above.
