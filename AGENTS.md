## Core Framework / 核心方法論

### The Chokepoint Principle
Don't buy the obvious beneficiary (NVDA, MSFT, META). Buy what they **must** depend on.
The archetype: AXTI + Sumitomo controlling ~60-70% of global InP substrates = the
"Strait of Hormuz" of AI photonics. When applied to a question about stocks, always ask:
*What does the headline name depend on, and who controls that supply?*

### Rotation Sequence (as of mid-2026)
```
Phase 1: HBM/Memory           ← DONE  (institutions fully in)
Phase 2: Optical Transceivers ← TAIL  (LITE, COHR, AAOI, AXTI mostly priced)
Phase 3: ELS / SiPh / LPK     ← NOW   (External Light Sources, glass core)
Phase 4: 800V DC Power Infra  ← EARLY (NVDA 800V push, Korean power equipment)
```
Entry edge = buying a phase before sell-side coverage begins.
Exit signal = when bank analysts get "passive-aggressive" about a call you made early.

### Stock Selection Criteria
- Market cap sweet spot: $400M–$5B (below institutional radar)
- Supply concentration: >50% global share of a specific physical input
- Confirmed hyperscaler customer dependency (disclosed or strongly inferred)
- "Pure play" preferred over diversified conglomerates
- Bucket A (asymmetric): AXTI, SIVE, AAOI-tier — chokepoint torque
- Bucket B (safe compound): TSEM, SOI, COHR-tier — defensible durability

### Position Sizing
- Starter size in micro/small cap: 0.5–1%
- Standard thematic: 5–10%
- Highest conviction (validated by ≥1 earnings cycle): up to ~30%
- Always pair high-beta names with blue-chip / broad ETF ballast
- **Rule**: Build conviction first or you will capitulate the bottom and chase the top

### Risk Management
- Baseline leverage: ~1.4x margin
- During geopolitical/macro stress: cut leverage to ZERO
- Drawdown tolerance: 15–25% single-day moves are noise, not signal
- Tactical hedge during crisis: ~25% FAZ + ~25% GUSH + short basket ("Doomsday ETF")
- Prop/HFT presence in holder list = expect engineered stop-loss cascades; don't use tight stops

### Entry & Exit
- Entry timing: before institutional coverage, not after
- Validation gate: wait for earnings/customer disclosures, not price action
- Each position has explicit PT + time horizon:
  - $NBIS $92 → PT $400 / 1Y | $RKLB $43 → PT $500 / 5Y
  - $CRCL $72 → PT $150 / 8M | $ALAB $143.4 → PT $250 / 6M
- Hold horizon: 2–5 years on conviction names
- Repricing lag is real: a confirmed thesis can take weeks-to-months to reprice after earnings

### Psychology
- "It's always when you're earliest to something that you get the most criticism/doubt."
- Treat "meme stock" labels as noise unless the chokepoint fundamental actually broke
- Stop watching X/Reddit during drawdowns — wait for the next earnings print
- Anti-copy-trade: he doesn't announce sells; don't blindly follow position entries either
- Calibrate expectations: 10%/day moves are not the baseline

### Research Process
1. Map the supply chain first (raw material → substrate → component → subsystem)
2. Identify which node has <5 global suppliers or multi-year substitution lead time
3. Confirm hyperscaler customer dependency (disclosed or strongly inferred)
4. Multi-order stress test: trace through 3rd–5th order effects (geopolitics, substitution)
5. AI pressure-test: feed thesis to an LLM and ask it to attack from all angles
6. Earnings = validation gate; scale conviction only after the print confirms
